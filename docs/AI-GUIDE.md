# AI Content Improver - Quick Start Guide

## What is it?

The AI Content Improver uses Google's Gemini 2.0 AI to analyze and improve text content on any webpage. It's perfect for:
- WordPress blog posts and pages
- Product descriptions
- Service pages
- Marketing copy
- Any web form with text fields

## Setup (One-time)

### 1. Get Your Free Gemini API Key

1. Visit https://aistudio.google.com/app/apikey
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Click "Create API Key" button
4. Copy the API key

### 2. Configure Your Brand Settings

1. Open the extension
2. Click "✨ Try AI Content Improver"
3. Fill in the Settings tab:
   - **API Key**: Paste your Gemini API key
   - **Brand Name**: Your business name (e.g., "Melbourne Fridge Repairs")
   - **Business Focus**: What you do (e.g., "Commercial refrigerator repairs")
   - **Target Audience**: Who you serve (e.g., "Restaurants, Hotels, Cafes")
   - **Service Area**: Where you operate (e.g., "Melbourne, Australia")
   - **Content Tone**: Choose your preferred style (Professional, Friendly, Casual, Formal, or Persuasive)
4. Click "Save Settings"

## How to Use

### Step 1: Navigate to Your Page
- Go to any webpage with text fields you want to improve
- Example: WordPress editor, form builder, CMS

### Step 2: Scan the Page
1. Click the extension icon
2. Open "AI Content Improver"
3. Go to "Improve Content" tab
4. Click "Scan Page & Generate Suggestions"

### Step 3: Review AI Suggestions
- The AI will analyze each text field
- You'll see:
  - **Original**: Your current text
  - **AI Suggestion**: Improved version
- Each suggestion is tailored to your brand settings

### Step 4: Accept or Reject
- Click "✓ Accept" for improvements you like
- Click "✗ Reject" for ones you don't want
- You can accept as many or as few as you want

### Step 5: Apply Changes
- Click "Apply All Accepted Changes"
- All accepted suggestions will be applied to the page
- The changes are made directly in the text fields

## Tips for Best Results

### Brand Information
- Be specific with your brand name and focus
- Include your unique selling points
- Mention your location for local SEO

### Content Tone
- **Professional**: For B2B and corporate clients
- **Friendly**: For consumer-facing businesses
- **Casual**: For young audiences and informal brands
- **Formal**: For legal, medical, or academic content
- **Persuasive**: For sales and marketing copy

### Target Audience
- Be specific about who you're targeting
- Include industry types (e.g., "Restaurants, Hotels")
- Mention demographics if relevant

## Example: Appliance Repair Business

**Settings:**
```
Brand Name: Citywide Appliance Repairs Melbourne
Business Focus: Commercial refrigerator and appliance repairs
Target Audience: Restaurants, cafes, hotels, and food service businesses
Service Area: Melbourne, Australia
Content Tone: Professional
```

**Original Text:**
"We fix fridges fast. Same-day service."

**AI Improved:**
"Fast, reliable commercial refrigerator repairs across Melbourne with same-day service. Our expert technicians keep your food service business running smoothly with minimal downtime."

## Troubleshooting

### "Please set your API key in Settings first"
- You need to add your Gemini API key in the Settings tab
- Get one free at https://aistudio.google.com/app/apikey

### "No text fields found on this page"
- The page doesn't have any editable text fields
- Make sure you're on a page with input fields or textareas
- Try opening the WordPress editor or form editor

### "API request failed"
- Check that your API key is correct
- Make sure you have internet connection
- Verify your API key hasn't expired

### AI suggestions seem off-brand
- Update your Settings with more specific brand information
- Change the Content Tone to match your style
- The more details you provide, the better the results

## Privacy & Security

- Your API key is stored locally in your browser
- Content is sent to Google's Gemini API for processing
- No data is stored on external servers by this extension
- You can delete your API key anytime from Settings

## API Costs

- Gemini API has a generous free tier
- Most users won't exceed the free limits
- Check current pricing at https://ai.google.dev/pricing

## Need Help?

- Try refreshing the page
